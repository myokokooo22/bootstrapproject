Our Services and Facilities

Business Service

Let us assist you with your business matters with our selection of support services and meeting spaces.

Fitness Center

Revitalise your senses with an invigorating workout at our 24-hour fitness centre.

Outdoor Infinity Swimming Pool

Located on the wellness floor, our outdoor infinity swimming pool offers one of the best photo-taking spots in Yangon.

St. Gregory Spa and Wellness Centre

St. Gregory offers a full range of wellness facilities, including a sauna, steam room, Jacuzzi, swimming pool, as well as a range of spa treatments for the ultimate indulgence.

Weddings

Create a wedding experience to remember at Pan Pacific Yangon. Conveniently located in the heart of the city, our hotel features a grand ballroom suitable for a lavish reception. With our team of event planners and culinary experts to guide you along the way, tailor your special day to make it a memorable occasion. Fitted with advanced audio-visual equipment, our event space can be customised to host an intimate reception or a grand celebration for up to 600 guests.
